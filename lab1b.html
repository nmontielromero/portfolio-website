My Favorite Banana Bread Recipe

During my freshman year of college I discovered my love for banana bread and learned to make it using the little kitchen utensils I had in my dorm room. It was on of my favorite things to do in my free tme!

More of B.B

    Ingredients

  • Bananas
  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla extract
  • Butter
  • Chocolate Chips Optional
  • Salt
  • Love

    Steps

  1. Preheat the oven 350F and prepare the pan
  2. Mash Bananas and add the butter
  3. Mix in remaining ingredients
  4. Put in the oven let it cool down and enjoy
